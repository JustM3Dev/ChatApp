Welcome to LanChat!

LanChat is a free open source web based chat application made just to focus on your privacy. You can download the server and host it on your own computer on Github. Your data is not being seen by any third parties.

About

This web-app was developed by JustMe. It was made using the following libraries: Bootstrap, Socket.io and JQuery.
